Crystals Collector!

To play:


  1. You will be given a random number at the start of the game."Number To Get"
  2. There are four crystals below.
  3. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
  4. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  5. You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
  6. Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each crystal.
